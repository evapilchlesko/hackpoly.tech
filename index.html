Hack Poly.Tech


About

Hack Poly.Tech is a 24-hour hackathon held at the Baltimore Polytechnic Institute on March 14-15, 2020 from noon to noon, run by the school's hacking and coding club, Poly.Tech.


Targeted for high school students interested in computer science in the greater Baltimore and Washington, DC area, Hack Poly.Tech is filled with workshops, activities, and coding all day (and night) long.
